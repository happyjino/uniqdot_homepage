Product introduction:

The InP-based Diagnostic Quantum Beads are products characterized by an emission wavelength of 620-630 nm, offering high binding strength and biocompatibility. These are amphiphilic polymer-coated, water-dispersible core/shell quantum dot groups. They are treated to allow the binding of bioactive materials such as antibodies or proteins that are specific to viruses and pathogens, making them suitable for use in applications such as LFA diagnostic kits for diagnosing various diseases.

4.
Characteristic
  • Composition: InP@ZnSeS@ZnS (core-shell) encapsulated with amphiphilic polymer.
  • Emission Wavelength: 530 nm~630 nm (options available)
  • Quantum Efficiency: ≥90%
  • Solvents: Polar solvents such as distilled water, PBS buffer solution, and serum.
5.
Applications
  • Bioimaging: Precisely targeting cell surface receptors for cancer cell diagnosis and imaging.
  • Lateral Flow Immunoassay (LFA) kits:Used in rapid diagnostics, enhancing sensitivity and reliability.
